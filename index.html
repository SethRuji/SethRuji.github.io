Movement

I am a mover with a focus on Parkour and Freerunning but also regularly train breakdancing, juggling, tricking, tumbling, and a variety of other movement arts.

Parkour & Freerunning

I have been training parkour since February 2011. In this time I have developed my skills through watching videos online and occassionally training with others. I have become very involved in attending and hosting jams every year. I am an American Parkour Ambassador to help positively promote parkour and a healthy lifestyle. I also coach parkour and freerunning part time at Top Flight Gymnastics and Swift Movement Studio.

Breakdancing

In 2013, one of my friends invited me to start learning to bboy. Shortly after that I was one of the founding members of a small club we started. As time went on I have learned a number of skills, I still consider myself a novice though.

Currently no videos =(

Juggling

While training parkour during the winter in my alma mater's gymnasium I became fascinated by the juggling club members who were practicing. Shortly there after, I joined the club and started to pick it up. I found it to be an excellent way to balance out my training as it has greatly improved my hand-eye coordination.
